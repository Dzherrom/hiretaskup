Frequently Asked Questions

Can I access TaskUp Assistant worldwide?
Yes — TaskUp works worldwide. We support clients across different time zones and adapt to your preferred working hours whenever possible.
What does “unlimited work” mean?
Unlimited work means you can assign as many tasks as you want within your plan’s weekly hours. Task completion time depends on task complexity and priority.
Do you provide 24/7 coverage?
TaskUp offers support during standard business hours based on your plan. If you need extended coverage, let us know and we’ll discuss custom availability options.
How do I communicate with TaskUp?

We adapt to your preferred communication channel.

You can communicate with your TaskUp assistant using the platform you already use in your company—such as Email, WhatsApp, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom/Google Meet, or any other tool your workflow requires.

No communication limits. We keep it simple and aligned with how your team already works.

Who are my assistants?
Our assistants are carefully selected and trained to support busy founders and teams. We focus on strong communication, reliability, and execution. You’ll be matched with an assistant based on your needs, working style, and time zone.
How long does it take to get replies?
Response times depend on your assistant’s working schedule and task priority. During active working hours, you can typically expect fast replies and clear updates. For urgent requests, we recommend marking them as high priority.
Can TaskUp integrate with software I use?
Yes. Your assistant can work with the tools you already use — such as Google Workspace, Slack, Trello, Asana, HubSpot, and more.
How does customer support work?
If you need help, you can contact us anytime by email or your preferred communication channel. We typically respond within 1 business day, and often much faster. Your assistant can also escalate urgent issues to our team when needed.
